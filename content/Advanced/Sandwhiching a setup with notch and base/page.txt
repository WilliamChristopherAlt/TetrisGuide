<div class="h1">Sandwiching Setup with Notch and Base</div>
<div class="text">
  Sandwiching a setup with notch and base is an advanced technique that involves
  placing one setup pattern between a notch structure and a base structure. This
  creates a layered pattern that enables multiple T-Spin opportunities.
</div>

---

<div class="h2">Notch and Base Structure</div>
<div class="text">
  The notch and base structure provides the foundation for sandwiching setups. The
  notch creates a cavity for T-Spin execution, while the base provides stability
  and additional pattern support.
</div>

<div class="h2">Sandwiching Technique</div>
<div class="text">
  When sandwiching a setup between notch and base, the middle pattern must be carefully
  positioned to maintain all three structures' integrity while maximizing scoring
