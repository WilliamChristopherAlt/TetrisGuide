<div class="article-title">Layering a setup on top of a setup</div>

<div class="h1">Layering Setups</div>
<div class="text">
  Layering a setup on top of another setup is an advanced technique that creates
  multi-level patterns. This approach maximizes board efficiency by stacking
  multiple T-Spin opportunities vertically.
</div>

---

<div class="h2">Layering Technique</div>
<div class="text">
  The layering technique involves building one setup pattern on top of another,
  creating a vertical stack of T-Spin opportunities. This requires careful planning
  to maintain both patterns' integrity.
</div>

<div class="h2">Multi-Level Execution</div>
<div class="text">
  Executing layered setups requires understanding the execution order and piece
  requirements for each level. Proper execution ensures maximum scoring from
  all layers.
</div>
