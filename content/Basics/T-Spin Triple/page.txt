<div class="h1">T-Spin Triple Setup</div>
<div class="text">
  The T-Spin Triple is an advanced technique that clears three lines with T-Spin bonus.
  This setup requires more precise stacking but offers excellent scoring potential.
</div>

---

<div class="h2">Triple Setup Pattern</div>
<div class="text">
  The T-Spin Triple setup creates a deeper cavity that allows the T-piece to clear
  three lines when rotated correctly. This pattern is more complex than the Double setup.
</div>

<div class="h2">Key Considerations</div>
<div class="text">
  When building a T-Spin Triple setup, ensure you have enough height and maintain
  the correct cavity shape. The setup can be vulnerable to misdrops.
