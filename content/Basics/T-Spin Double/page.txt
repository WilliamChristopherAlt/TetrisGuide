<div class="h1">T-Spin Double Setup</div>
<div class="text">
  The T-Spin Double is one of the most common and useful T-Spin techniques. It clears
  two lines while providing significant bonus points.
</div>

---

<div class="h2">Basic Setup Pattern</div>
<div class="text">
  The classic T-Spin Double setup requires creating a cavity with a notch on one side.
  The T-piece is then rotated into this space to clear two lines simultaneously.
</div>

<div class="h2">Execution</div>
<div class="text">
